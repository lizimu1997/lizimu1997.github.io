SURVEY OF USERS & STAFFS IN THE SERVICE AREA

Survey of users and staffs



We interviewed practitioners and service personnel in several service areas. They were asked about the current problems and their expectations for the service area.The result reflects a series of deficiencies and potential development trends in the existing service areas.


At the same time, these interviews were organized into word clouds. In order to see their needs more intuitively.




“Carrying out long-distance cargo transportation across the country, and having to return to the truck to rest after eating at the high-speed service station. Put a simple bed behind the truck. Sometimes only eat instant noodles in the car to save money and time..”


— Truck driver


“Don’t have long driving experience, so always need to go to the service area to rest. If there is a quiet, hygienic and convenient rest room,hope to use it. Hope to have a comfortable and enjoyable process when go out to work or travel.”


— Car driver


“Generally work at night to manage the safety of the parking lot and its surroundings. There are frequent thefts from late night to morning, which is very hard work.”


— Security


“Convenience stores are open from 7:00 in the morning to 10:00 at night, and there is a lot of traffic on holidays and weekends. Although a driver’s lounge was set up, it was transformed into a storage room. Usually rest in his car.”


— Clerk


Summary & Goals



It can be seen from the above data that China’s highway construction is developing very rapidly, but at the same time there are still some shortcomings. At the same time, users also put forward different expectations and requirements.

Government Construction

PAST

The Chinese government has completed the design and development of China's highways in nearly 40 years. The infrastructure is already very complete, but the supporting facilities need to be improved.

Investment

NOW

It is hoped that these data can be used to explain to investors the current development of China's highways, attract investors to invest, and further strengthen the construction of China's highways.

FUTURE
